About Neil

I'm a web developer with 15 years experience. I'm based in the rural town of Otley, which is North Leeds.

My first web development job was for London Agency www.tui.co.uk back in 2000. Back then, clients we're BT, The Halifax and Asda. The web was fresh, new and exiting. I learned my trade here and focussed on the backend with PHP and MySql.

Since then I've built many projects ranging from small brochure sites to full blown content management systems for global brands like Wagamama. I've been a senior developer a number of agencies including engageinteractive.co.uk who are based in Leeds and work as a consultant and developer on a freelance basis for hypetype.co.uk (Los Angeles), hellolovestudio.com (London), humaan.com (Perth, Australia), bluestone98.com, madebytall.co.uk and zeppocreative.com.

I've been involved in all aspects of project development from planning through to development, deployment and performance tweaking. I'm as happy in the back end as I am in the front end. At one company I was called 'head of digital' although that seemed all a little too over the top for me.

I'm comfortable with most aspects of web development. I offer full stack development, in Ruby (Ruby on Rails) / PHP (Custom / Magento / Wordpress) and front end work CSS (Sass, Less) / HTML5 / Javascript (Jquery / Backbone) and all work is fully responsive. I'm also experienced at writing a technical spec and pitching.

I'm currently interested in Javascript heavy frameworks like react.js and angular.js but I'm not sure which one I'm going to concentrate on yet.
